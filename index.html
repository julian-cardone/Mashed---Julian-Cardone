Instructions


Welcome to Mashed! This program mashes up songs. Select a song from the instrumental list, and one from the vocal list. You are able to play each audio individually and change their beats per minute. Once you are ready to mash, press the 'Match BPMs!' button. This will link the two songs, allowing you to easily change their speed using the vocal slider (on the right side). If you run into any issues simply refresh the page. Enjoy!
